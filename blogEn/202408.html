Three requirements for astral projection

The first requirement for astral projection is to meditate before going to bed. I try to meditate for 20 minutes in a surf chair because I fall asleep easily in the sleeping position. The second requirement is to concentrate mentally and feel the flow of the winds or drops in the central channel. This is because the winds of the central channel become the astral body. The third requirement is to meditate on emptiness without thinking just before falling asleep. This will break the attachment between the winds and the outside world, allowing the astral body to leave the physical body at the same time we fall asleep.

Personal Experience Last night, around 3:30 a.m., after sleeping twice, I sat in a surf chair and concentrated on the tip of the genitals. I felt the wind in the central channel and the drops of the head dripping down. Then, I went into a sleeping position and meditated on emptiness without thinking. An astral projection then occurred in the room, which ended after a short while.
