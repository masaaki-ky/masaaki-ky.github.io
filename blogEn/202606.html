My first out-of-body experience since moving to Thailand

I have decided to leave my job in Japan and relocate to Chiang Mai, Thailand. The reason behind this is a series of revelations brought about by various events. Nine years ago, I moved from Yokosuka to Shizuoka for a similar reason, which turned out to be an incredibly rewarding decision, so I chose to follow the guidance once again.

During a second sleep in the middle of the night, I practice a meditation where I bend my knees and lift my hips. After a while, when I raise my body, I feel myself float up and confirm that I have detached from my physical form. The room I find myself in is different from the reality I know. Since my astral body is unstable, I move in circles around the room while repeatedly throwing straight punches to anchor and stabilize my ethereal self. Once I feel my astral presence steadying, I notice a large mirror in the room and look into it. I see a dark-complexioned face and a pouch hanging around my neck. Inside the pouch are medicinal tablets. I take just one. Next, I peek outside through the window and see several people walking out of a building. At that point, the astral projection comes to an end.
